# Sales Analysis Dashboard using SQL and Power BI

## Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data from the Sample Superstore dataset using SQL and Power BI. The objective is to identify sales trends, profitability drivers, regional performance, customer segment behavior, and business opportunities through interactive dashboards and data-driven insights.

---

## Business Problem

Businesses need to understand which products, regions, customer segments, and states contribute the most to sales and profit. By analyzing historical sales data, organizations can make informed decisions to improve profitability, optimize operations, and support strategic growth.

---

## Dataset Information

* Dataset: Sample Superstore
* Records: 9,994 transactions
* Data Type: Retail Sales Data

## Key Insights

### Category Performance

* Technology generated the highest sales and profit.
* Furniture achieved strong sales but lower profitability.
* Office Supplies maintained stable profitability.

### Regional Performance

* West Region generated the highest sales.
* East Region was the second-largest contributor.
* South Region recorded the lowest sales.

### Customer Segment Analysis

* Consumer Segment contributed the highest revenue.
* Corporate customers remained an important revenue source.
* Home Office customers generated the lowest sales.

### State-Level Performance

* California was the top-performing state in both sales and profit.
* New York ranked second in overall performance.

### Discount Analysis

* Average discount across transactions was approximately 16%.
* Higher discount levels may negatively impact profitability.

---

## Business Recommendations

* Increase investment in Technology products.
* Improve Furniture pricing and discount strategies.
* Expand successful sales strategies from the West Region.
* Strengthen customer retention for Consumer Segment customers.
* Focus marketing efforts on high-performing states.
* Monitor discount policies to protect profitability.
